Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our basic PDF translation service is completely free. We may offer premium features in the future.

How long does translation take?

Translation time depends on document size and complexity. Most documents are processed in 1-5 minutes.

Is there a file size limit?

Currently, we support PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, consider splitting them first.

What languages do you support?

We support all major world languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and more.

Is the translation accurate?

Our translations are powered by advanced AI technology that provides high accuracy, but for critical documents we recommend professional human review.
